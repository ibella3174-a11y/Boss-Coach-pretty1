Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: March 28th, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We believe in strict data minimization. We only collect the necessary information required to orchestrate the Glamour ecosystem effectively:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Full Name, Email Address, Username, and heavily encrypted secure Passwords provided directly during the Premium registration phase.
  • Platform Usage Data: Analytical data relative to your interactions with European counterparts, digital portfolio uploads, platform navigation paths, and time-on-site metrics.
  • Device & Log Information: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, and timestamp data essential for monitoring security integrity and preventing fraudulent login attempts.

2. Utilization of Data

Your protected data is deployed strictly to optimize and maintain your Glamour profile. It is utilized to:

  • Power the algorithmic pairing system, matching you dynamically with relevant European users or global gig opportunities.
  • Process secure financial transactions seamlessly via our certified partner, Korapay.
  • Monitor analytical progress within Glamour Skill Up and dynamically unlock physical or digital fitness/platform rewards.
  • Deliver critical administrative communications, policy updates, and security alerts directly to your inbox.

3. Information Sharing and Third-Party Subprocessors

Glamour does not sell your personal data to data brokers. We share limited architectural data strictly with essential infrastructure partners:

  • Google Firebase: Utilized for robust, enterprise-grade authentication, database management, and encrypted user data storage.
  • Korapay: Utilized exclusively for PCI-compliant financial processing and secure checkout flows.
*These authorized subprocessors adhere to rigorous international privacy standards.

4. Cookies and Tracking Mechanisms

Our platform utilizes essential cookies—small data files stored locally on your device—to maintain your authenticated session states and remember premium user preferences. By utilizing Glamour, you consent to the deployment of these fundamental session cookies.

5. Security & Data Retention

We enforce industry-standard cryptographic security protocols (including SSL/TLS transit encrypting) to guard your personal information against unauthorized access. We retain your personal data strictly for as long as your premium account is active.

Important: While we deploy high-tier security architectures, inherent internet transmissions cannot be guaranteed 100% secure. You provide your data at your own calculated risk.

6. Your User Rights

As an empowered user of the Glamour ecosystem, you hold the definitive right to view, modify, extract, or request the total deletion of your Personal Identifiable Information directly from your authenticated dashboard. You may also opt-out of promotional communications at any time.
